About Myself

I believe in a powerful combination of teamwork, technology, and active lifestyle.

  1. Times of individual unicorns are behind us; nowadays challenges are best fought in teams.

  2. Technology should not make us lazy. Technology makes our lives easier and richer by empowering us to focus on things that matter most.

  3. Active lifestyle makes our mind, body and soul act as one, and gives us the power to change the world through advancement in technology and teamwork.



My passion is turning value adding technological advancements into exciting business opportunities. I like to understand how things work, but even more, how does our World benefit with them.

Two of my preferred technologies are AI and blockchain, both on the verge to change the way we live. I expect AI solutions to improve our psychological and physical health, whereas distributed networks should help us depend less on subjective individuals. By combining both technologies we should get the best of both Worlds and improve our safety.
